About Sherab

Sherab Eazy Dorji is a Bhutanese entrepreneur, musician, and youth leader. He is the founder and CEO of EchoReach Directories, a platform that supports local businesses through digital marketing, directory listings, TikTok promotion, SEO, and media services. Sherab is also a rapper and creative artist, deeply involved in storytelling through music, writing, and cultural engagement. He is known for his introspective style, inspired by raw life experiences and Buddhist values.

EchoReach Directories

EchoReach is a pioneering initiative in Bhutan that offers the following services:

  • Directory Listings for Businesses
  • Social Media and TikTok Promotion
  • Logo Design and Branding Packages
  • Business Proposal and Plan Writing
  • Blogging, SEO, and Front-End Development
  • Digital Marketing Services

The platform supports economic growth by connecting businesses with customers and enabling visibility in the digital space.

Career Highlights

Vision
