About Sherab

Sherab Eazy Dorji is a Bhutanese entrepreneur, musician, and youth leader. He is the founder and CEO of EchoReach Directories, a platform that supports local businesses through digital marketing, directory listings, and media services. Sherab is also a rapper and creative artist, deeply involved in storytelling through music and writing.

Career Highlights

  • Founded EchoReach Directories – a business network empowering SMEs in Bhutan
  • Former CEO of Eazy Record Company
  • Collaborated with artists like MC Mikey, Jikme the Carriage, and Alu Tshagay
  • Active in social campaigns including drug awareness and youth mobilization events
  • Planned Bhutan's first billboard initiative in Thimphu

Vision

"My work is not just about business or music. It's about telling the truth, representing unheard voices, and building platforms that help people rise."
